=== AUGMENTED GRAMMAR ===

Expr' -> Expr
Expr -> Expr + Term | Term
Term -> Term * Factor | Factor
Factor -> ( Expr ) | id

=== FIRST SETS ===

First(Expr') = [(, id]
First(Expr) = [(, id]
First(Term) = [(, id]
First(Factor) = [(, id]

=== FOLLOW SETS ===

Follow(Expr') = [$]
Follow(Expr) = [$, ), +]
Follow(Term) = [$, ), *, +]
Follow(Factor) = [$, ), *, +]
